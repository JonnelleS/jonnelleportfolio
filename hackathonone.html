Who We Are

Our team, Help4theWild, is a well known organization that works towards gaining recognition that wildlife deserve to be loved by great families not just domestic animals. We are here to provide homes for these adorable ferrets and do our best to help find the best fit for them.

The Client

Our client Bob from Bobs Burgers has been urged by his edgy teenage daughter, Tina, to adopt an adorable ferret. Tina is looking for a cool dude ready to explore the world with her and attract the most attention from the popular kids in school.
